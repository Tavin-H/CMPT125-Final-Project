## Extra Features

We have a boolean called use_colour that, when set to true, makes the pieces
have colour; however, apparently not all terminals support the colour, so it
may not work for certain users.

The game over text is made with ASCII art, to see it properly, please resize
your terminal to fit the text.

## Limitations and Known Bugs

The bot isn't very smart and doesn't see any moves ahead, it can't detect when
the player could win with an anvil, not that the bot could stop the player anyways,
because the anvil isn't a very well designed custom piece.
